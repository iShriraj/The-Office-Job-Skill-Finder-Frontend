Welcome to Skill Finder

Your education and experience may make you eligible to apply for a job but, to be successful in most roles, you will need skills that you are likely to develop over time. Some will be specific to the job. Graduate employers place a lot of emphasis on finding candidates with the right skills and competencies for their organisations. Depending on the career sector and profession you choose to work in, there could be very specific skills, abilities and knowledge needed to do the job.

Skill finder will find skills which are most important for a specific job position. It will search through internet , The skills you should posses or the skills you should learn for a job of your choice.


All you have to do is Enter " What Kind of Job " you want and " Where Do You Want " it to be !!!
